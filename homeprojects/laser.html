Laser Engraving (and cutting)

In 2023, my wife bought a laser engraver and we've both had a blast learning how to use it. We have both been talking about reserving a booth at a convention or other craft show to sell some of our work. We haven't gotten that far yet. Hopefully soon.

I won't share any of my wife's stuff here, because she'll just make me look bad.


Designs

Most of these designs were done in Photoshop and/or Procreate. They're made through different methods: AI generations via Bing (usually edited afterwards), modifications of existing images, or created by me. My creations are usually made using reference material. More info on that in the Procreate/Photoshop section.

Wood

Engravings on basswood. This material came with the printer, so it's where I started.

Stone

A couple stone coasters. I haven't done much on these coasters because I didn't want to "waste" them.

Metal

Metal business cards that came with the printer. I also bought some license plates for gifts. I really like these. Unfortunately, they don't photograph the best. They're about 10x cooler in person.

Coasters

Some cheap material to make some cool designs.

Laser Cutting

I've only done a couple of laser cutting projects so far. The Charlie Brown I made as a gift using some internet reference material and drawing in Procreate. The life counter is a design from Etsy.
