Photoshop and Procreate

I'm lumping these together as a general "digital art"-type category. They are similar, but Procreate is more for me to draw with the Apple Pencil and Photoshop is usually me editing images with a mouse. I've also dabbled in several other applications like Affinity and Inkscape.


Photoshop

I usually like to do subtle edits, like taking text out of a movie poster and turning it into a background or something similar, but I've done a little bit of everything, I think.

Images are resized for download speed purposes.

Procreate

I'm still relatively new to Procreate, but it's quickly becoming my favorite.

Procreate Timelapses

Procreate allows you to save timelapse videos of your creations. Have a look!
