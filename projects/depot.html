Elections Depot

The elections depot was our warehouse for elections equipment and my "home away from home" so to speak.

For the majority of my time in Linn County, I was in charge of the building, including operations within and organization.


Operations

For bigger elections, we brought in a team of temporary workers to help pack materials and test equipment for about 80 locations. This generally lasted 2 months at a time.

On paper, I was never in charge of these workers, but in reality, I managed basically all aspects of their work other than pay. I would provide materials, guidance on procedures, and answer any questions they had during their time there.

Organization

Early 2020, I became the sole permanent employee in this location. I used the opportunity to reorganize pretty much everything in order to increase efficiency.

Materials were separated by where they needed to go and materials were separated so we could update, replace, and inventory things as needed.
