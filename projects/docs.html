Documentation

Since we trained hundreds of workers each election from varying experience levels and backgrounds, documentation was an important part of keeping elections workers aware of what needed to be done.

I took on making clear and concise guides for technology, procedures, and issues that may arise during election day.

Picture Guides

Guides meant to look like emergency instructions on an airplane.

Guides on Applicable Materials

This guide was printed on the envelope that the materials went in. No need to look it up.

Equipment Guides

We had some guides on correctly setting up and taking down equipment on 11x17 sheets.

Checklists

Chairpersons for election voting locations had a few checklists to verify everything was good to go.

Full Guides

Some things can't easily be summed up. These had reference materials given in a binder of instructions. Some of these examples are just excerpts.
